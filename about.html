About¶

The ETE Toolkit was originally developed at the bioinformatics department of CIPF (Valencia, Spain), and greatly improved at the comparative genomics group of CRG (Barcelona, Spain) and the Structural and Computational Biology unit of EMBL (Heidelberg, Germany).

At present, ETE is maintained by the Comparative Genomics and Metagenomics group at CBGP (Madrid, Spain).

Citation:

ETE 3: Reconstruction, analysis and visualization of phylogenomic data.

Jaime Huerta-Cepas, Francois Serra and Peer Bork.

Mol Biol Evol 2016; doi: 10.1093/molbev/msw046

Highlighted Tools Using ETE¶

  • EggNOG (orthology database)

  • PhylomeDB (phylogenetic database)

  • Polyphony (3D structures comparsion)

  • Phylemon (phylogenetic tools)

  • Species delimitation (method)

  • TreeKO (duplication aware method to compare trees)

  • AGP (Alignment-Free Genome Phylogenies)

  • ITEP (exploration of microbial pan-genomes)

  • T-RMSD (protein structural classification)

  • CanSNPer (genotype classifier of clonal pathogens)

  • ReproPhylo (reproducible phylogenetic analyses)

  • Avocado (Linux automated testing of tools)

  • StreptomeDB 2.0 a database of natural products produced by streptomycetes

  • IQ-TREE web server: fast and accurate phylogenetic trees under maximum likelihood.
